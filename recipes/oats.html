Oats Meal

Description

This is one of the easiest heavy meal you can have at home.

Ingredients

  • Oats of any kind from the grocery store
  • Blackberries, honey, nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil some water
  2. When it's boiling, add your oats
  3. Stir until it's cooked and soft
  4. Add some sugar and taste
  5. Leave it out to cool, and add some milk, honey, and blackberries
