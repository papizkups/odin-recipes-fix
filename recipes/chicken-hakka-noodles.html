Chicken Hakka Noodles

Description

This is one of the easy tasty meals you can make at home.

It also doesn't takek too much time.

Ingredients

  • Noodles of any kind
  • sliced Cabbage, Capscicum, slightly boiled carrots
  • sliced pieces of boiled chicken
  • grinded pepper (optional)
  • soy sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil your noodles till it's done
  2. Add a little oil while it's boiling, so it doesn't stick together.
  3. drain the water out, and leave it to cool for 5mins
  4. Take out your pan. add a oil, and roast your sliced cabbage, carrots, and capsicum
  5. Add your spices (maggi, salt, white pepper)
  6. Add the boiled noodles, and roast them all together for about 3mins
