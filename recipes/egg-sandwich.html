Egg Sandwich

Description

A complete balanced breakfast

Ingredients

  • Raw eggs
  • Sliced Bread

Steps

  1. Toast each slice of bread till it's golden brown
  2. Get your pan with a little oil till it's slightly hot
  3. Break your eggs whole without spilling the york
  4. Once the egg white turns white, use a spatula spoon to get it out.
  5. place between each slice of bread
